## Laser cut parts
We recommend cutting these parts in 20 gauge (~0.9mm) stainless steel (both 316 and 304 are fine).  If you use a stock thicker than that and the steel gate insert won't fit in the gate.  We cut ours at [Laser Bros](https://laserbros.com/), they did a great job!
